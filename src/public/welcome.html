Capabilities

Everything your treasury needs

Six integrated modules that replace a team of humans with one autonomous engine.

Multi-Protocol Yield

Automatically routes capital to the highest-yielding position across Kamino, marginfi, Solend, and Jupiter Lend — rebalancing every 60 seconds.

Smart Liquidity Engine

Maintains configurable reserve buffers while maximising deployed capital. Withdrawals are handled programmatically — no manual intervention needed.

Scheduled Payments

Define recurring payroll, vendor disbursements, or grant schedules in USDC or USDT. Executes on-chain at precisely the configured interval.

Live Dashboard

Real-time portfolio view with APY breakdowns, historical yield curves, allocation heat-maps, and upcoming payment queue — all in one console.

Immutable Audit Trail

Every allocation decision, yield accrual, and payment is recorded on-chain. Export a cryptographically verifiable ledger at any time for compliance teams.

API-First Design

Full REST and WebSocket API. Integrate StableDesk into your existing ERP, accounting software, or bespoke trading system in hours, not weeks.

Process

Up and running in three steps

From wallet connection to autonomous operation in under ten minutes.

1. Connect

Link your Solana wallet or create a dedicated program-derived treasury address. StableDesk never holds private keys — only the instructions you approve are executed.

2. Configure

Set your target APY, acceptable protocol list, liquidity reserve ratio, and payment schedule through a guided UI — or push a JSON config via the API.

3. Operate

The on-chain scheduler takes over. Monitor live allocations, override any position instantly, and receive Slack or webhook notifications on every significant event.

Security

Security by design, not afterthought

Every architectural decision starts from the assumption that no party should be trusted — including us.

Non-Custodial Keys

Your wallet's private key never leaves your device. StableDesk uses program-derived addresses and signed instruction bundles — we can authorise nothing without your signature.

Tamper-Proof Audit Log

Every state transition is an on-chain transaction. No database, no middleware, no mutable server log. A regulator or auditor can independently verify every decision from genesis.

On-Chain Transparency

Program source code is open and verifiable on-chain. Any stakeholder can inspect the exact logic governing capital allocation without trusting StableDesk's word.
